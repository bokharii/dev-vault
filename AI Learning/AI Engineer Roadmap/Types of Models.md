* AI models come in various forms
	* **open** models provide transparent access to their architecture and training data
	* **closed** models keep those details proprietary
	* **pre-trained** models are trained on massive datasets and can be fine-tuned for specific tasks, saving time and resources
	* **self-hosted** models offer greater control and privacy as they are deployed on your own infrastructure

* **Pre-trained** models are Machine Learning (ML) models that have been previously trained on large datasets to solve a specific task or set of tasks. They can be further fin  tuned or adapted for other related tasks. Serves as a good starting point as you don't have to train a new model from scratch
